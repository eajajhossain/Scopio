🔭 Scopio Lead Finder

Find business leads → send to your Scopio account

This extension will:

  • Run a business search in your Scopio account (free — OpenStreetMap + Geoapify).
  • Talk only to the Scopio server URL you set.
  • Store your settings locally in this browser only.

No API key needed. No background tracking — it only acts when you click “Find leads”.

Connect your Scopio account to start (no API key required).
